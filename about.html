About Us

"We pick out everything your dog needs in order to be head of the pack."

Pick of the Pack is a subscription box that puts together a personalized box for your dog every month with a fun theme. Each box contains at least two toys, treats, some sort of chew, some other dog related accessories or products and sometimes something for the dog’s human!
Products and goods are all natural and locally based.
10% of the profit of each box is also donated to a carefully selected charity.

The box is shipped at the beginning of each month for free!
